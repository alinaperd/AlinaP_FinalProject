By Alina Perdomo

This project was inspired by the work of one of my favorite artists, Magdiel Lopez. I admire his ability to always come up with inventive concepts and visuals, and the fact that he is a self-taught artist. In this project, you will see three surreal portraits I created with Adobe Photoshop. While I started with fairly traditional portraits I found on Pexels, I added shapes, textures, colors, lighting, and animation, to create surreal and whimsical images.
